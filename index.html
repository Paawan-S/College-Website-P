    LNCT Group of Colleges is The Best Educational Professional Group in Central India LNCT Synonymous with excellence in higher education with 32+ Years of Academic Excellence & Discipline.

    Today, LNCT Group is one of the Largest and most preferred Educational Group for Engineering, Management, Pharmacy, Medical with ISO-9001 certification & NBA accreditation for many Engineering courses.. Bright young students from all across the country are coming to fulfill their dream in this group.

    Born in semi urban city of Bilaspur (C.G.),Shri Jai Narayan Chouksey, completed his post graduation from Robertson College, Jabalpur. He established Lakshmi Narain College of Technology (LNCT), Bhopal in 1994 under the banner of H.K. Kalchuri Education Trust. Since then, With the commitment of “Working Towards Being the Best”, LNCT Group has achieved many milestones. The institute is successfully meeting the objective of producing skilled manpower of the highest quality able to cope up with the challenges of ever evolving industrial needs of our country. The Alumni of LNCT Group are working all across the Globe in Big Multi National Companies.

    The LNCT Campus at Bhopal is spread over a lush green environment of 50 acres (approx.). Our Campus constitutes of many buildings that house academics & research centers. In addition to this, The academic building has tutorial rooms, lecture halls, computer and electronics labs, offices of the administrations as well as The faculty rooms. The campus is fully equipped with facilities like Wi-Fi,central library (digital library, Internet lab), digital classrooms, hostel, mess,Central workshop, Auditorium,Sports Ground etc.
